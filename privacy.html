Privacy Policy

How Wimpy Books collects, uses, and protects your data.

What we collect

We collect the information needed to create and manage your account, process uploads and purchases, and synchronize your reading progress.

  • Name and email address
  • Book upload metadata and cover images
  • Reading progress and purchase history
  • Authentication tokens for session management

How we use it

Your information is used to:

  • Authenticate and sync your dashboard
  • Store your uploaded books and cover images
  • record and protect your reading progress
  • Keep your experience personalized across sessions

External services

We may use third-party services for authentication, payments, and storage. Any service-specific data handling is governed by the provider’s policies.

Security

We use standard security practices to protect your data and keep your account secure. Sensitive keys and tokens should be stored safely and never shared publicly.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, reach us through the contact page.
